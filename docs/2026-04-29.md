### 라우팅 흐름
```txt

  -> ROUTES.home("/")
    -> <AppShell />
      -> <AppHeader />
      -> <Outlet />
      -> <BottomTabBar />
```

    - `status !== "authenticated"`이면 `<Navigate to={ROUTES.login} />`

    - `/purchase`
